Futures Trading Using Margin and Cryptocurrencies

Futures trading allows buying or selling cryptocurrencies at a set price in the future, profiting from price changes without owning the asset. Using margin trading, I borrow funds to amplify trades, e.g., $100 controls $1,000 with 10x leverage, but it’s risky due to potential losses or liquidation. I focus on meme coins (like DOGE or PEPE) and BTC to bet on price movements.

Hyperliquid Network

Hyperliquid is a decentralized exchange (DEX) where users trade directly on a blockchain, without intermediaries. Its Hyperliquid L1 blockchain supports fast, low-cost trading with up to 200,000 orders per second and no gas fees. I use Hyperliquid for its speed, security, and support for meme coins and stablecoins with up to 40x leverage.

MetaMask Wallet and Fund Management

I use MetaMask, a digital wallet, to store cryptocurrencies and connect to Hyperliquid. Funds are converted to Ethereum or USDC and sent to the trading wallet. Withdrawals involve converting tokens to USDC on Hyperliquid’s spot market and optionally swapping to fiat on centralized exchanges like KuCoin or Coinbase

The Trading Bot and Strategy Development

My custom Python trading bot automates buying and selling on Hyperliquid using predefined strategies. Strategies use technical indicators (e.g., RSI, Bollinger Bands) to trigger buy/sell signals. I backtest strategies with historical data and use machine learning to optimize settings, tailoring them for Hyperliquid’s high leverage and unique features.
