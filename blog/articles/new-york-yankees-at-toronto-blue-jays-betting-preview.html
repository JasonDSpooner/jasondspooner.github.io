New York Yankees at Toronto Blue Jays: Betting Preview

Published: June 12, 2026

The Toronto Blue Jays host the New York Yankees at Rogers Centre in an American League East clash.

The Lines

Sports Select has priced the matchup as a near coin-flip:

  • Moneyline: Blue Jays +135 | Yankees +145
  • Run line: Blue Jays -0.5 (-132) | Yankees +0.5 (-125)
  • Total: 8

Toronto is a slight favorite on the run line, reflecting home-field advantage in what oddsmakers expect to be a tight game.

The Angle

With the moneyline this close, the value sits on the run line rather than picking a side outright. The Blue Jays -0.5 ticket at -132 only requires Toronto to win by one run, and the home matchup against a left-handed starter points toward the home side.

Bottom Line

Look for Toronto to take the opener against the Yankees. The recommended play is Blue Jays -0.5 (-132).
