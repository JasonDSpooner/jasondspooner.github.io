MLB Preview: New York Yankees at Toronto Blue Jays

Published: June 12, 2026

The Toronto Blue Jays host the New York Yankees on Friday, June 12, 2026, at Rogers Centre.

The Lines

Sports Select has priced the series opener as a near coin-flip:

  • Moneyline: Blue Jays +135 | Yankees +145
  • Run line: Blue Jays -0.5 at 1.76 | Yankees +0.5 at 1.80
  • Total: 8

Toronto is a slight favorite on the run line, reflecting home-field advantage in a game the oddsmakers expect to be tight.

The Angle

With the moneyline this close, the value sits on the run line rather than picking a side outright. The Blue Jays -0.5 at 1.76 is the better play: they are at home, face a left-handed starter, and only need to win by one run to cash the ticket.

Bottom Line

Look for Toronto to take the series opener against the Yankees. The recommended play is Blue Jays -0.5 at 1.76.
