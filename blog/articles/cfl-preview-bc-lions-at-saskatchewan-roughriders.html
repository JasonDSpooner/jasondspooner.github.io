CFL Preview: BC Lions at Saskatchewan Roughriders

Published: June 12, 2026

The BC Lions head to Regina to take on the Saskatchewan Roughriders on Saturday, June 13, with kickoff at 7:00 p.m. MT at Mosaic Stadium.

The Odds

Sports Select sees this as a tight contest. The Roughriders are slight home favorites at +110 on the moneyline, with the Lions at +155. The point spread is Saskatchewan -2.5, and the total is 52.5.

The Pick

Saskatchewan +110 on the moneyline offers the best value. The market is pricing the game close to a coin flip, and the home-field advantage in Regina is worth siding with. The -2.5 spread is also playable if you want a little insurance.

The total sits at 52.5, which suggests the books expect a moderately high-scoring game. With both teams priced close together, the moneyline is the cleaner bet.
