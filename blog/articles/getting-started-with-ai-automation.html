Getting Started with AI Automation in Your Workflow

Published: June 12, 2026

As a CTO managing multiple projects and teams, I'm always looking for ways to work smarter, not harder. Over the past year, I've integrated AI tools into my daily workflow, and the results have been transformative.

Why AI Automation?

The promise of AI isn't about replacing humans—it's about amplifying what we can do. By automating repetitive tasks, we free up mental bandwidth for creative problem-solving and strategic thinking.

Tools I Use Daily

Here are the AI tools that have become essential in my workflow:

  • Code assistants for writing and reviewing code faster
  • Writing assistants for drafting documentation and reports
  • Data analysis tools for processing sports data and financial metrics
  • Workflow automation for scheduling and task management

Getting Started

If you're new to AI automation, start small. Pick one repetitive task in your workflow and find an AI tool that can help. Measure the time saved, then expand from there.

The key is to view AI as a teammate, not a replacement. When used thoughtfully, these tools can dramatically increase your output while maintaining quality.
