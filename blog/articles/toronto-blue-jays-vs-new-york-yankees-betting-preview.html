Toronto Blue Jays vs New York Yankees: Betting Preview

Published: June 13, 2026

The Toronto Blue Jays continue their American League East series with the New York Yankees on Saturday afternoon at Rogers Centre. After taking the opener 8-5 on Friday, the Jays will look to build momentum against a Yankees club that remains within striking distance of the division lead.

The Lines

Sports Select has priced Saturday's matchup as a one-run game on the run line and total:

  • Run line: Yankees -0.5 (1.70) / Blue Jays +0.5 (1.86)
  • Total: 7.5 runs — over 1.80 / under 1.80

We are skipping the proline moneyline because MLB extra innings can produce a tie outcome, making the run line and total the cleaner betting angles.

The Angle

The tight run line tells the story: oddsmakers see this as a one-run game either way. With the Blue Jays coming off a win in the series opener and playing at home, the +0.5 cushion offers a better value play than trying to navigate the extra-innings tie factor on the proline. The Yankees are a quality club, but their bullpen has been taxed in recent days and the Rogers Centre crowd should give the Jays an edge in a close contest.

The total of 7.5 also looks low given both lineups have shown power in this series. Friday's game saw 13 runs cross the plate, and with warm summer conditions expected in Toronto, another high-scoring affair is in play.

The Pick

  • Blue Jays +0.5 run line (1.86) — small value on the home side in a game priced too close to call.
  • Over 7.5 (1.80) — both teams have swung the bats well in this series, and the total has not adjusted enough.

Odds via Sports Select as of the morning of June 13, 2026.
