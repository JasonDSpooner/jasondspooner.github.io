Toronto Blue Jays vs New York Yankees: Betting Preview

Published: June 13, 2026

The Toronto Blue Jays continue their American League East series with the New York Yankees on Saturday afternoon at Rogers Centre. After taking the opener 8-5 on Friday, the Jays will look to build momentum against a Yankees club that remains within striking distance of the division lead.

The Lines

Sports Select has priced Saturday's matchup as a near coin flip:

  • Blue Jays (home): 2.50
  • Tie: 2.95
  • Yankees (visitor): 2.30

On the run line, the Yankees are listed at -0.5 (1.70), while the Blue Jays sit at +0.5 (1.86). The total is set at 7.5 runs, with both the over and under offered at 1.80.

The Angle

The tight moneyline tells the story: oddsmakers see this as a one-run game either way. With the Blue Jays coming off a win in the series opener and playing at home, the slight underdog price on Toronto offers the better value. The Yankees are a quality club, but their bullpen has been taxed in recent days and the Rogers Centre crowd should give the Jays an edge in a close contest.

The total of 7.5 also looks low given both lineups have shown power in this series. Friday's game saw 13 runs cross the plate, and with warm summer conditions expected in Toronto, another high-scoring affair is in play.

The Pick

  • Blue Jays moneyline (2.50) — small value on the home side in a game priced too close to call.
  • Over 7.5 (1.80) — both teams have swung the bats well in this series, and the total has not adjusted enough.

Odds via Sports Select as of the morning of June 13, 2026.
