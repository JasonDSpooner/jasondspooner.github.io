NY Mets vs St. Louis Cardinals: A Betting Preview with Moneyline Recommendation

Published: June 12, 2026

The New York Mets face off against the St. Louis Cardinals in a crucial MLB matchup on June 11, 2026, at 7:10 PM ET. With both teams fighting for playoff positioning, this game offers compelling betting opportunities, especially when examining the moneyline odds.

Game Overview

The Mets, currently sitting at a modest record, are looking to bounce back after a recent slump. Their offensive struggles have been evident, particularly in run production, which has put pressure on their pitching staff to carry the team. Meanwhile, the Cardinals, despite being slightly better in the standings, have also faced challenges in recent games, showing inconsistency in both pitching and hitting.

According to Sports Select, the betting line for this matchup shows the Mets as a slight underdog with odds of +100, while the Cardinals are listed at +175. This implies that bettors expect a relatively close contest between these two teams, though the Cardinals' higher odds suggest some confidence in their ability to win.

Spread and Total Analysis

While the moneyline presents an interesting opportunity, the spread line reveals a more nuanced picture. The spread is set at -0.5, meaning the Cardinals are favored by half a run. The spread odds are -185 for the Mets and +109 for the Cardinals. This suggests that the bookmakers believe the Cardinals can win by at least one run, but it's not a heavy favorite.

In terms of the over/under, the total is set at 9 runs, indicating that the game should see a moderate amount of scoring. Both teams have shown some offensive capability, but neither has been dominant enough to consistently score high totals.

Betting Recommendation

Given the relatively even betting lines and the lack of a clear favorite, the most compelling bet here is on the moneyline. The Mets’ odds of +100 represent a fair value proposition, especially considering their recent form and the fact that they're playing at home. While the Cardinals are the better team overall, the Mets have shown resilience and could take advantage of a favorable matchup.

If you’re looking for a safer play, the Cardinals at +175 offer solid value, particularly if you believe they can capitalize on their stronger lineup and pitching performance. However, for those seeking a more aggressive bet, the Mets at +100 provide a balanced risk-reward ratio.

Conclusion

This game represents a classic case of two evenly matched teams with different strengths and weaknesses. While the Cardinals are slightly favored, the betting market suggests a competitive affair. For punters looking to place a strategic bet, the moneyline presents the best opportunity, especially with the Mets’ +100 odds offering a solid return on investment if they can pull off a win.
