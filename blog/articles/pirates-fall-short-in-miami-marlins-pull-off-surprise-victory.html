Pirates Fall Short in Miami, Marlins Pull Off Surprise Victory

Published: June 13, 2026

In a game that defied pregame expectations, the Pittsburgh Pirates were defeated 8-3 by the Miami Marlins on Friday, June 12, 2026. The result marked a surprising upset for the Marlins, who were listed as +265 underdogs in the moneyline odds provided by Sports Select.

Game Summary

Miami’s victory came off the back of a strong offensive performance, scoring runs in multiple innings to build a commanding lead. Pittsburgh’s pitching struggled to contain the Marlins' attack, particularly in the middle innings where Miami capitalized on defensive miscues and weak at-bats from the Pirates’ lineup.

The Marlins’ ability to plate runs consistently throughout the contest proved decisive, especially with their balanced approach at the plate and aggressive base running. While Pittsburgh managed a few scoring opportunities, they were unable to capitalize on key situations.

Odds and Expectations

Prior to the game, the Marlins were heavily favored to lose, with Sports Select offering odds of +265 on the Pirates. The over-under for the game was set at 8.5 runs, but the final total was 11, indicating a high-scoring affair. The game's outcome was a notable departure from the betting market's expectations, which had the Pirates as significant underdogs.

Takeaway

This game serves as a reminder of the unpredictability of baseball, where a team's recent form and betting line can be misleading. Despite being labeled as a long shot, the Marlins’ execution and resilience in a high-scoring game underscored their potential to compete against higher-ranked opponents. For bettors, this result highlights the importance of looking beyond simple odds when evaluating team performance and momentum.
