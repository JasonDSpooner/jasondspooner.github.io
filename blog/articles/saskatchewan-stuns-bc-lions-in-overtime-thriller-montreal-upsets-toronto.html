Saskatchewan Stuns BC Lions in Overtime Thriller, Montreal Upsets Toronto

Published: June 14, 2026

In a thrilling weekend of Canadian Football League action, the CFL delivered a trio of captivating matchups that kept fans on the edge of their seats. The Saskatchewan Roughriders emerged victorious in a dramatic overtime clash against the BC Lions, while the Montreal Alouettes stunned the Toronto Argonauts in a high-scoring affair. Meanwhile, the Hamilton Tiger-Cats powered past the Winnipeg Blue Bombers in a defensive battle.

Saskatchewan Roughriders Edge BC Lions in Overtime

The most surprising result came from the Saskatchewan Roughriders, who overcame a 10-point deficit in the fourth quarter to defeat the BC Lions 31–27. Despite being favored by 125 points according to Sports Select odds, the Lions failed to hold their ground, leaving fans wondering if the underdog Roughriders had the edge all along. The game was a back-and-forth affair with both teams trading touchdowns, but Saskatchewan’s defense held strong in the closing minutes.

Montreal Alouettes Defeat Toronto Argonauts

In another shocking upset, the Montreal Alouettes outlasted the Toronto Argonauts 37–30, defying pre-game expectations. The Alouettes’ offense dominated the first half, building a commanding lead that the Argonauts couldn’t overcome despite a late push. The win puts Montreal in a stronger position in the standings and highlights their improved form this season.

Hamilton Tiger-Cats Defeat Winnipeg Blue Bombers

The Hamilton Tiger-Cats secured a hard-fought victory over the Winnipeg Blue Bombers 37–27, showcasing their offensive prowess and defensive resilience. While not as surprising as the other two games, Hamilton’s performance reinforces their status as a top contender in the East Division.

Betting Takeaways

While the odds suggested a relatively predictable outcome in the Roughriders vs. Lions matchup, the game served as a reminder that in the CFL, anything can happen. The underdog victory by Saskatchewan may have been a surprise to some, but it reflects the league’s unpredictable nature and the importance of execution in close contests. Betting on the underdog in the CFL can pay off—especially when momentum and form are on their side.

As the CFL season progresses, these results set the stage for an exciting playoff race. With each game offering new storylines and surprises, fans can expect more drama in the weeks ahead.
