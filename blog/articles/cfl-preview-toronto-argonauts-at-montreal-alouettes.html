CFL Preview: Toronto Argonauts at Montreal Alouettes

Published: June 12, 2026

The Toronto Argonauts travel to Montreal to face the Alouettes on Friday, June 12, with kickoff set for 7:00 p.m. MT at Percival Molson Memorial Stadium.

The Odds

Sports Select has the Alouettes as clear favorites. Montreal is listed at -213 on the moneyline, while Toronto sits at +290. The point spread is Montreal -7, and the total is 51.5.

The Pick

Montreal -7 is the logical play. The Alouettes are priced like a team that should control the game at home, and a touchdown cushion is reasonable against a Toronto side that is expected to struggle on the road.

If you prefer the total, the line at 51.5 is balanced at 1.80 on both sides, so there is no obvious lean from the market. Take Montreal to cover the spread.
