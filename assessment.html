Critical Assessment & Verification Summary

Risk Assessment Matrix

Critical Risk Level

  • Agency Identity Unverified: No business registration, website, or client references found across multiple search strategies. No case studies, no client references, empty appendix for metrics.

High Risk Level

  • Sector Specialization Mismatch: Outdoor-adventure expertise claimed for vintage/retro business
  • Template-Based Construction: Self-admitted template with placeholders, no client-specific analysis
  • Contractual Protection Absent: No terms, conditions, or recourse framework presented

Verification Methodology & Findings

Search Strategies Employed

  • Exact Agency Name Search: No direct matches found
  • Geographic Specification: Slovakia, Canada, US regional searches
  • Industry Directory Search: No professional association listings
  • Related Entity Analysis: No demonstrated connections to similar-named entities

Template Construction Evidence

"This report template is ready to customize for any specific client" — Direct quote from proposal document. Placeholder pricing fields, template contact information, empty case study appendix.

Required Risk Mitigation Actions

Before Any Engagement

  • Business Registration Verification: Request official business registration, tax identification, principal identification
  • Client Reference Validation: Demand verified references with contact permission for direct verification
  • Detailed Pricing Structure: Require component-level pricing with explicit scope boundaries

Contractual Protections Required

  • Formal Contract Documentation: Comprehensive terms with milestone deliverables and acceptance criteria
  • Performance Guarantees: Negotiate service level agreements or performance-based fee structures
  • Termination Rights: Clear exit provisions with data portability and transition assistance
