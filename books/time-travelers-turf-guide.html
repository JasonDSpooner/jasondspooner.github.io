Creative Non-Fiction · Published on Amazon

A field guide for the chronologically adventurous — a walk through time, place, and the landscapes that shaped them.

The Time Traveler's Turf Guide blends history, geography, and storytelling into a unique travel companion. Whether you're exploring ancient streets, forgotten battlefields, or the layered history beneath modern cities, this guide helps you see the past alive in the present.

Inside you'll find:

  • Historical deep-dives tied to real-world locations
  • Guided narrative walks through significant eras and events
  • Tips for experiencing history firsthand at living sites
  • Stories that connect past and present in unexpected ways

For curious travelers, history buffs, and anyone who's ever wished they could step into another era.
