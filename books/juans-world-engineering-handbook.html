Juan's World Engineering Handbook

Technical Reference · Published on Amazon

A comprehensive engineering handbook built for real-world problem solvers.

Juan's World Engineering Handbook distills practical engineering knowledge into an accessible, no-nonsense reference. Covering foundational principles, modern tools, and hands-on techniques, it's designed for engineers, technicians, makers, and anyone who builds things that need to work.

Inside you'll find:

  • Core engineering principles explained without the fluff
  • Practical formulas, calculations, and reference tables
  • Real-world applications and troubleshooting frameworks
  • Guidance on materials, structures, systems, and safety

Whether you're studying engineering, working in the field, or tackling DIY projects that require real rigor, this handbook gives you the tools to think and build like an engineer.
