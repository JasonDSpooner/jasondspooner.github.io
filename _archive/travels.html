The Redwood Forest

One of the most profound trips I've taken — standing among the oldest living things on Earth.

I went to the Redwood Forest once. That sentence doesn't capture the scale of it. Walking among trees that have lived for over a thousand years puts everything in perspective — your career, your worries, your place in time.

Travel is a core part of how I think and create. Whether it's a road trip with my partner, a solo hike, or just finding a new view, those moments fuel the work I do back at the keyboard.

I post travel content on YouTube — playlists of trips, sports betting adventures, and the whimsical side of life.
