What I Do

Music is a universal language, and the right soundtrack transforms an event from ordinary to memorable. I combine technical audio knowledge with crowd-reading intuition to build experiences that people remember.

Services

Live DJ Events

Sports events, private parties, corporate functions, and venue residencies. Reading the room and adapting the set in real time.

Crowd-Powered Tools

Interactive systems that let audiences vote on songs, request tracks, and shape the playlist through their phones.

Curated Radio

Custom streaming stations and playlist curation for brands, venues, and online communities.

Audio System Design

Consulting on speaker placement, mixer configuration, and signal flow for small-to-medium venues.

Event Types

  • Corporate team-building and holiday events

What I Learned

Being a DJ is part technician, part psychologist. You can have the best equipment and the deepest music library, but if you can't read a room and pivot when the energy drops, none of it matters. The technical skills get you in the door. The ability to make people feel something keeps you there.
