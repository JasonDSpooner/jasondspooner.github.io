2D to 3D Transformation

A unique service I offer is transforming line art and illustrations into physical 3D-printed objects. The process involves:

  • Importing 2D illustrations into CAD software
  • Extruding and sculpting depth and detail
  • Generating printable meshes with proper wall thickness and supports
  • Printing in resin or FDM depending on detail requirements
  • Post-processing: sanding, painting, and finishing

This bridges my illustration work with manufacturing — turning digital creativity into something you can hold.

What I Learned

3D printing is 20% technology and 80% patience. Bed adhesion, temperature towers, retraction settings — the learning curve is steep but rewarding. Teaching others has made me a better troubleshooter, because explaining why a print failed forces you to truly understand the physics.
