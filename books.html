The Therapeutic Power of Coloring

Research Thesis · 2020 – Present

An in-depth thesis examining the scientific evidence behind coloring as a legitimate wellness practice — from its historical roots in art therapy to modern applications in clinical and everyday settings.

The research covers mental health benefits (anxiety reduction, stress relief, meditative states), cognitive development (focus, flow states, motor skills), and creative engagement across all age groups.
